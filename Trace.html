Trace of Matrix

Trace of a Matrix:

In a square matrix, the sum of elements of the principal diagonal is called the 'trace of a matrix'. We denote the trace of the matrix A by 'tr A'.

Note:
The trace exits only for Square Matrices.
Example:

Trace(A)=a+e+i
